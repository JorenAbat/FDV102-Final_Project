About the Film

The creation of a modern masterpiece

Makoto Shinkai

Makoto Shinkai is a Japanese animator, filmmaker, and manga artist known for his emotionally powerful stories and stunning visual style. Born in 1973 in Nagano Prefecture, Shinkai began his career creating video games before transitioning to animation.

His distinctive style, characterized by beautiful skyscapes, detailed urban environments, and themes of connection and separation, has earned him recognition as one of Japan's most innovative animators.

Production

Animation

The film was produced by CoMix Wave Films, known for their high-quality animation and attention to detail. The team used a combination of traditional hand-drawn animation and digital techniques to create the film's stunning visuals.

Music

The soundtrack was composed by RADWIMPS, who created both the background music and the theme songs. The music plays a crucial role in conveying the emotional depth of the story.

Success

Released in 2016, "Your Name" became the highest-grossing anime film worldwide and received widespread critical acclaim for its storytelling, animation, and emotional impact.
