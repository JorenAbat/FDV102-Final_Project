Main Characters

The story of two souls connected across time and space

Mitsuha Miyamizu

宮水 三葉

A high school girl living in the rural town of Itomori. As the eldest daughter of the Miyamizu family, she helps her grandmother maintain their family shrine. Tired of her small-town life, she wishes to be reborn as a handsome Tokyo boy in her next life.

  • Age: 17
  • Location: Itomori
  • Occupation: High School Student

Taki Tachibana

立花 瀧

A high school boy living in Tokyo. He has a part-time job at an Italian restaurant and aspires to become an architect. His life changes dramatically when he starts switching bodies with Mitsuha.

  • Age: 17
  • Location: Tokyo
  • Occupation: High School Student / Part-time Waiter
